FAQS ON IBADAN TALENT HOUSE

Most Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the most frequently asked questions you may check before getting started

What is Ibadan Talent House?

A Community driven non profit given to the purpose of training the next generation builders. The long term vision is to be an Entrepreneurial House from a Talent House in the next five years. We want to build a talent pipeline that can reach everyone. We are starting one baby step at a time by training people how to design websites, animation and data science. we hope to be able to train people in different skills as time goes by partnering with other non-profit and schools alike.

Our Missions and Goals?

Our main mission is to improve the talent pipeline. We will achieve this mission by training, research and building. We will also partner with foundations to train children inrural areas in Ibadan. We want to be the bridge to those without.

Why Ibadan?

According to the UN-Habitat in 2022, Ibadan is currently the second fastest growing city in Africa . And we hope to scale up the talent pipeline and also effectively accelerate this process. We believe we can give more definition to the growth of this pacesetter city. As much as the current governmentis building great projects around of the city. We also want to add our quota of development to this ongoing building by improving the talent pipeline. And also Ibadan has the potential to facilitate this great project.

Why Non-profit?

We are really interested in the good of the future and we are invested in the growth of the people .We decided to remove all forms of profit.The only price you pay is your willingness to learn.

What do we need to achieve this?

A space to train people
Laptops to help people who can't afford it
Stable internet and Electricity
We need a lot of help, advice and support because this is a new project we believe we are trying to do in a different way
